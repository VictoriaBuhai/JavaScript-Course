This app is just a demo version of some real movie platforms: Netflix, TMDB, SweetTV and so on. Movies are gotten from TMDB (using Api); What this app can do:

  • If you want to add film to favorites, you need to press the button "add..." at the bottom of the card
  • After pressing the "Add..." button, it changes to "Remove from favorites"
  • You can check if the movie is already added to your list of favorites (go to Home page)
  • You can remove movies from the favourites list by clicking "remove..."
  • If you didn't find your movie, you can use search
